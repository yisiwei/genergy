    Outstanding Battery Series

    • UPS High Power NH Series
      Use in:uninterrupted power supply system

      NH series adopts lead calcium alloy grid, with high rate lead paste formula and sophisticated production techniques. Thanks to the stability in large power discharge performance, the series serves excellently in emergency power supply systems.

      detail
    • Deep Cycle EV Series
      Use in： electric vehicles incl. Golf cart

      Equipped with high-tin & low-calcium alloy grid and motive used paste formula, EV series has much better deep cycle performance than regular deep cycle batteries. The series is widely used in golf cart, sightseeing bus and power tools.

      detail
    • Gel Deep Cycle NG Series
      Use in: photovoltaic energy system

      NG series is a combination of new deep cycle lead calcium alloy grid, long life active material formula and Nano-Gel technology. Designed for solar and wind energy system, the series has outstanding performance of over-discharge under low temperature environment.
